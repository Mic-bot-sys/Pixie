    Piexie is also an excellent example of aligning with your brand by emphasizing your core values. The page kicks off with a simply stated Purpose, Vision, Mission Statement, and Brand Manifesto. But as you can see, these sections are kept as brief as possible – in other words, there’s no fluff!


    I could highlight a lot of things about fashion company Natori’s About Us page – the well-crafted story, the abundance of white space – but my favorite element of this page is its simple social media integration. The interactive Instagram feed at the bottom of the page is a great way to show off a ton of products without clogging up the rest of the page with too many pictures, and the trendy photos give us even more insight into the brand’s ethos.
